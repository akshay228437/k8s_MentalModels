# PersistentVolume (PV) – Kubernetes Mental Model

A PersistentVolume (PV) is a **cluster-wide storage resource** that represents real physical or cloud-backed storage.

It is **not namespace-scoped**, and is consumed by Pods via a PersistentVolumeClaim (PVC).

Think of it as:
- PV = actual storage
- PVC = request for storage

---

# Full YAML Structure

```text
PersistentVolume
├── apiVersion: v1
├── kind: PersistentVolume
├── metadata
│   ├── name: my-pv
│   └── labels
│       └── type: fast-ssd
│
└── spec
    ├── capacity
    │   └── storage: 10Gi
    │
    ├── volumeMode: Filesystem | Block
    │
    ├── accessModes[]
    │   ├── ReadWriteOnce (RWO)
    │   ├── ReadOnlyMany (ROX)
    │   └── ReadWriteMany (RWX)
    │
    ├── persistentVolumeReclaimPolicy: Retain | Recycle | Delete
    │   ├── Retain → manual cleanup required
    │   ├── Recycle → basic scrub (deprecated)
    │   └── Delete → deletes underlying storage automatically
    │
    ├── storageClassName: fast-storage
    │
    ├── mountOptions[]
    │   ├── nfsvers=4.1
    │   ├── hard
    │   └── timeo=600
    │
    ├── nodeAffinity
    │   └── required
    │       └── nodeSelectorTerms[]
    │           └── matchExpressions[]
    │               ├── key: kubernetes.io/hostname
    │               ├── operator: In
    │               │
    │               └── values[]
    │                   └── node-1
    │
    └── persistentVolumeSource
        ├── nfs
        │   ├── server: 10.0.0.10
        │   └── path: /exported/path
        │
        ├── awsElasticBlockStore
        │   ├── volumeID: vol-0abcd1234
        │   └── fsType: ext4
        │
        ├── gcePersistentDisk
        │   ├── pdName: my-disk
        │   └── fsType: ext4

        ├── azureDisk
        │   ├── diskName: my-disk
        │   └── cachingMode: ReadWrite
        │
        ├── hostPath
        │   └── path: /data/pv
        │
        └── csi
            ├── driver: ebs.csi.aws.com
            ├── volumeHandle: vol-123456
            └── fsType: ext4
